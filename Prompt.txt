### **Core Logic Requirements**

#### **1\. Standard Production**

For cards with a fixed value (like *Nautilus* or *M.E.R.V* ), the production is simply quantity \* value.

#### **2\. Complex Production**

The function must implement the following variable scoring logic based on the owned cards (playerCardQuantities):

* **Ancient Gate:**  
  * Production is equal to the **maximum quantity** of any single, unique card name the player owns. This includes the *Ancient Gate* itself.  
  * *Example:* Player owns 2 *Ancient Gate* and 5 *T-Wing*. Max quantity is 5\. **Each** *Ancient Gate* produces 5\.  
* **The Collective:**

* **Yggdrasil:**

* **Transit Hub:**  
  * Production is equal to the **count of unique roll\_values** represented by all cards the player owns.  The roll value range is 2-8.

  * The roll value of the **Transit Hub itself (8)** must be included in the count.

#### **3\. Exclusion of Gameplay Abilities**

Cards with abilities like *Beacon Hub*, *Fabricator Belt*, *Seraph Gate*, and *Deepspace Scouts* do **not** have production effects and are only relevant for their fixed value (if they have one).
